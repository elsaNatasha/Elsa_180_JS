The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is ad distinct style of garden that uses an informal design, dense plantings, and a mixture of ornamental and edible plants.

The Cottage Garden originated in England and its history can be traced back for centuries, although they were re-invented in 1870's England, when stylized versions were formed as to the more structured and rigorously maintainedd English estate gardens.

The earliest cottage gardens were more pratical than their moden descendants, with an emphasis on vegetavles and herbs, along with some fruit tress.
